Tic Tac Toe

Fun level: 3/10


A Quick Guide


This game is played between two players. The players alternate on turns. Player 1 has a default move of 'X' and player 2 has a default move of 'O'. This is a competition between player 1 and 2 to complete a row, a column, or a diagonal with their assigned letters.
